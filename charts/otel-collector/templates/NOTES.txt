The OpenTelemetry Collector (ilert OTLP gateway) is installed.

1. Check it is running:

   kubectl --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} get pods -l "app={{ include "otel-collector.name" . }}"

2. In-cluster clients (OBI, your apps) should send OTLP to:

   OTLP/HTTP : http://{{ include "otel-collector.fullname" . }}.{{ .Release.Namespace }}:{{ .Values.ports.otlpHttp }}
   OTLP/gRPC : http://{{ include "otel-collector.fullname" . }}.{{ .Release.Namespace }}:{{ .Values.ports.otlpGrpc }}

   Within the same namespace you can use the short name
   `{{ include "otel-collector.fullname" . }}:{{ .Values.ports.otlpHttp }}`.

3. Traces are forwarded to: {{ .Values.ilert.endpoint }}

4. Confirm the forwarder is healthy (no auth/connection errors):

   kubectl --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} logs -l "app={{ include "otel-collector.name" . }}" --tail=50

Now install the `obi` chart (or point your apps) at this collector, and your
services will appear on the ilert Service Health page.
